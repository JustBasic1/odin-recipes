One Pan Lasagna

Description

Another recipe for one pan lasagna. Takes 2 hours, but only 15 minutes of active time!

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Cheese
  • Ground Beef

Steps

  1. Add cooked ground beef to bottom of casserole pan
  2. Mix in tomato sauce and top with cheese
  3. Add pasta sheet to top of first layer
  4. Repeat steps 1-3 until out of pasta
  5. Enjoy!
