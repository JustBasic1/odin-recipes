Perfect Pizza Dough

Description

Here's a tasty recipe to make the perfect doughy and crispy pizza dough. Light and crispy and works for all humidities and temperatures!

Ingredients

  • All Purpose Flour
  • Water
  • Salt
  • 00 Flour
  • Olive Oil

Steps

  1. Combine dry ingredients
  2. Add wet ingredients and stir to combine
  3. Let rise for 15-30 minutes
  4. Knead for 5 minutes
  5. Wrap in fridge for 24 hours
  6. Roll out by hand and bake!
  7. Enjoy!
