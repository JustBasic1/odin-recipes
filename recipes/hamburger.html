Smash Burger

Description

Here's a tasty recipe to knock your socks off! A combination of ingredients to make the perfect smash burger without setting the smoke alarm off!

Ingredients

  • Buns
  • Tomatoes
  • 80/20 Ground beef
  • Lettuce
  • Cheese
  • Ketchup

Steps

  1. Roll ground beef into balls
  2. Heat pan over medium heat
  3. Add oil
  4. Place a pair of balls onto the pan and smash them down
  5. Flip a patty and add cheese to the back
  6. Flip the other patty onto the cheese
  7. Add smashed patties to the bottom bun
  8. Assemble the rest of your ingredients
  9. Enjoy!
